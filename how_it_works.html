Basic Functions of the Smell PGH App

  • You install the app onto your smart phone or tablet
  • At the first launch, the app will take you to the settings tab
    • You can choose to enter personal info, which will go to the Allegheny County Health Department (ACHD) with every smell report you submit, so that they can follow up with you if needed
    • The "Settings" tab also lets you choose the type(s) of app notifications you want
  • When you experience a pollution odor outdoors, you launch the app and pick a rating for the smell you experience (from 1 through 5)
  • Adding a description of the smell (e.g. "Industrial", "Woodsmoke", etc.) is highly encouraged, since this can help the county isolate the source
